Drain tips

Blog topics homeowners actually search for

Short education sections like these can grow into helpful blog posts and local search pages over time.

Why does my sink keep backing up?

Recurring sink backups may come from hair, soap film, toothpaste, grease, or a blockage farther down the line.

When should I call for a slow shower drain?

If water pools around your feet or the clog returns after basic cleaning, professional drain clearing can prevent a bigger backup.

What causes floor drain backups?

Laundry lint, dirt, debris, and main line buildup can all affect floor drains, especially in utility and laundry areas.

Can drain machines clear tough clogs?

Properly sized drain cleaning machines can break through buildup and restore flow when plunging or store products do not solve it.
